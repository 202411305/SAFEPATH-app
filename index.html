What SafePath Does

SafePath helps students and staff locate rooms, offices, elevators, stairs, restrooms, gates, and emergency exits across multiple floors. During emergencies, choose your current location and tap Emergency so the system can route you to the nearest exit.

How To Use The System
  1. Open Navigate and select your current location in From.
  2. Select your destination in To, even if it is on another floor.
  3. Turn on PWD Mode if you need a route that avoids stairs.
  4. Tap Find Path, then use the floor buttons to inspect each route segment.
  5. For emergencies, tap the red Emergency button to locate the nearest available exit.

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I find a route? ▾
Go to the Navigate tab. Select your starting point and destination from the dropdowns, then tap Find Path. The shortest route will be highlighted on the map.
What is PWD Mode? ▾
PWD (Person with Disability) Mode recalculates your route to avoid stairs and prioritize ramps and elevators, ensuring an accessible path across campus.
What is Emergency Mode? ▾
Emergency Mode checks how many people are already assigned to each exit. If the nearest exit is full, SafePath suggests another available way.
What is A* vs Dijkstra? ▾
Dijkstra's algorithm guarantees the absolute shortest path. A* uses a heuristic (estimated distance to the goal) to find paths faster, but may not always be the shortest. Both are good options.
I forgot my password. What do I do? ▾
On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your registered email or phone number. You will receive a one-time code to reset your password.
